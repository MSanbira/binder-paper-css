Variable Control

All measurements scale from the global font size variable. Use the slider above to see how the entire design scales proportionally.

Direction Support

The design automatically adapts for right-to-left languages. Click the RTL button to see the margin line and holes flip sides.

Custom Styling

You can override any of the CSS variables to customize the appearance: line thickness, hole position, colors, spacing, and more.

Large Heading

Large headings take up multiple line spaces to maintain the grid alignment. This ensures visual consistency across different content types.

Medium Heading

Medium headings are sized to fit the line rhythm while providing clear hierarchy and visual distinction from body text.

Small Heading

Small headings are underlined and fit within a single line space, perfect for subsections and detailed organization.
